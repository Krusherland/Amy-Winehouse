Grammy Awards

At the 50th Annual Grammy Awards in 2008, Amy won five Grammys, tying the record for most wins by a female artist in a single night at the time. These included:

  • Record of the Year: "Rehab"
  • Song of the Year: "Rehab"
  • Best New Artist
  • Best Female Pop Vocal Performance: "Rehab"
  • Best Pop Vocal Album: Back to Black
