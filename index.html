Her story

Amy Winehouse was a talented British singer-songwriter, known for her soulful voice and eclectic style that blended jazz, blues, and R&B. Hits like "Rehab" and "Back to Black" showcased her unique vocal style and candid lyrics, drawing from her personal struggles with addiction and relationships.
